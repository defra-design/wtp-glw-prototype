Version 1

Implemented changes
  • Digitised Annex VII form
  • Targeted user/persona (generator/broker)
  • User has successfully logged into the service by "some means"
  • The purpose of the service is prenotification only
  • The service offers no tracking of waste
  • Assumed prenotification is of Green List Waste only
Known limitations and issues
  • Maximum of three carriers can be added in this prototype
  • Currently unable to add more than one "transit" destination
  • No error messaging/validation has been included in this initial version
  • When "changing" entered data from the check your answers page:
    • Any radio button decisions are not presently brought through
    • The 'type ahead' entries are not presently brought through
    • The user will be forced to revisit all pages in the journey before being taken back to the check your answers page
