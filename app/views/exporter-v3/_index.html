Version 3

Implemented changes
  • Introduction of dashboard
  • Linear data gather replaced with 'task list'
  • Revised content audit
  • Concept of 'templates' has been introduced
  • Ability to update weight, date of shipment, and first carrier has been introduced
  • Address patterns: with England, Northern Ireland, Scotland and Wales options offered when international isn't applicable
  • Carriers: looping mechanism with interstitial list of 'carriers added' has been introduced
  • Multiple transit destinations looping mechanism has been added
  • GOVUK orientation page has been introduced
  • Defra ID account creation and account sign in, which includes Government Gateway, has been included
Known limitations and issues
  • Maximum of three carriers can be added
  • Maximum of three transit destinations can be added
